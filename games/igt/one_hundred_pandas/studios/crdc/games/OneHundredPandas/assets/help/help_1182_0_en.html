How To Bet
100 Pandas™ features 100 paylines. Choose to play 1, 10, 25, 50, 75 or 100 lines.

LINE BET
Displays the current bet per line shown in currency. Press the left arrow (-) to decrease the bet per line. Press the right arrow (+) to increase the bet per line.

LINES
Press the left arrow (-) to decrease the number of paylines played. Press the right arrow (+) to increase the number of paylines played.

SPIN
Press to spin the reels and submit the bet.

Auto Spin Feature
Auto Spin plays the game automatically for a selected number of spins at the current number of lines and line bet. Select the desired number of lines and line bet, press AUTO SPIN and choose how many spins to play. Auto Spin plays the specified number of spins until the STOP button is pressed, a Bonus is triggered, or the balance is insufficient to spin again.

Auto Spin is only available in approved jurisdictions.

Stacked Wilds™ Feature
With the Stacked Wilds™ feature, each reel in the base game is loaded with groups of four or more consecutive Wild symbols.

In the Free Spins Bonus, the Stacked Wilds™ feature is richer and more plentiful.

[ top ]


Free Spins Bonus
Three Bonus symbols in any position on the centre three reels trigger the Free Spins Bonus and award 2x the total bet.

Triggering the Free Spins Bonus awards ten free spins. The reels in the Free Spins Bonus have a richer, more plentiful Stacked Wilds™ feature than the base game reels.

During the Free Spins Bonus, the Bonus can be retriggered by getting three Bonus symbols anywhere on the centre three reels. Retriggering the Free Spins Bonus awards an additional ten free spins. The Bonus can be retriggered multiple times, up to a maximum of 255 free spins per Bonus.

The bet in the Bonus is the same as on the reel spin that triggered the Bonus.

Rules
Malfunction voids all pays and plays.

All line pay symbols must appear on a played line and on consecutive reels, beginning with the far left reel.

Only highest winner paid on each payline. Line wins are multiplied by the line bet on the winning line.

Scatter pays are multiplied by the total bet.

Coinciding wins on different paylines are added. Scatter awards are independent from payline awards and are also added to the total amount paid.

Awards are shown in currency.

Bonus symbol appears on reels 2, 3, and 4 only.

Regardless of the bet size, there is an award cap on any single transaction. See the paytable for details. A transaction includes the results of the Free Spins Bonus plus the outcome which launched the Bonus. If the award cap is reached in the Bonus, the Bonus ends immediately irrespective of any remaining free spins.
