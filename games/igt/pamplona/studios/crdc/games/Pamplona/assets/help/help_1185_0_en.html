How To Bet

Displays the current coin value as currency. Press the left arrow (-) to decrease the coin value. Press the right arrow (+) to increase the coin value.

SPIN
Press to spin the reels and submit the bet.

Auto Spin Feature
Auto Spin plays the game automatically for a given number of games at 1024 ways and the current coin value. Press AUTO SPIN to choose how many games to play at the current bet settings. Auto Spin plays the specified number of games until STOP button is pressed, bonus is triggered, or balance is insufficient to spin again.

Auto Spin is only available in approved jurisdictions.

MultiWay Xtra™ Feature
With the MultiWay Xtra™ feature, there are 1024 ways to win on every spin. MultiWay Xtra™ symbol awards pay for matching symbols in any position in adjacent columns, starting from the leftmost column. More than one matching symbol in the same column multiplies the award. The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except for the Bonus Scatter Pay symbol. One or more Wild symbols in a winning way doubles that winning way. Winning ways can only be doubled once in this manner.

Example of a MultiWay Xtra™ payout:
The following diagram shows a sample reel outcome, using the Fan symbol. Because there is at least one Fan or Wild symbol in each of the first four columns, the player wins the 4 Fan symbol award, which pays 50 coins per way.

Payout example:

Column 1 Column 2 Column 3 Column 4 Column 5
  Fan      
Fan   Fan Fan  
  Wild      
    Wild    
1 Fan 2 Fan 2 Fan 1 Fan

To determine the payout for this combination:
  1. Count the total number of winning ways (1 x 2 x 2 x 1 = 4).
  2. Count the number of non-wild winning ways (1 x 1 x 1 x 1 = 1).
  3. Calculate the number of wild winning ways (4 - 1 = 3).
  4. One or more Wild symbols in a way doubles the award. Multiply the number of wild winning ways by 2 (3 x 2 = 6).
  5. Add the number of non-wild winning ways with the number of wild winning ways (1 + 6 = 7). This example reel outcome will pay the 4 Fan symbol award times 7.
  6. Calculate the award amount in currency by multiplying the award (50 coins) by the coin value. For example if the coin value is £0.02 then the award is £1.00 (50 x £0.02).
  7. The total pay is the award value times the total number of winning ways (£1.00 x 7 = £7.00).
The win message will be: MULTIWAY XTRA™ WIN PAYS: £1.00 X 7

El Toro Bonus
Trigger the El Toro Bonus by getting three or more Bonus scatter symbols anywhere on the reels. These symbols are found on all reels.

Triggering the El Toro Bonus gives the opportunity to choose the number of free spins and the maximum multiplier. Choose from the following bonus options:
5 Free Spins with up to a 20x multiplier
10 Free Spins with up to a 15x multiplier
15 Free Spins with up to a 10x multiplier
20 Free Spins with up to a 5x multiplier
30 Free Spins with up to a 3x multiplier

Retrigger the bonus with three or more Bonus scatter symbols anywhere on the reels. These symbols are found on all reels. Each time the bonus is retriggered choose the number of additional free spins and the maximum multiplier for the additional bonus round. The bonus can be retriggered up to a maximum of three times in the same game.

Free spins are played at the same coin value as the triggering game.

Rules
Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

MultiWay Xtra™ awards pay for the same symbol in any position in adjacent columns.

Same symbol in same column multiplies award.

MultiWay Xtra™ awards pay left to right.

Only the highest paying MultiWay Xtra™ award for each symbol is paid per spin.

MultiWay Xtra™ awards are multiplied by the coin value.

One or more Wild symbols completing a symbol win double its pay.

Only the highest scatter pay award is paid. Scatter pays are multiplied by the coin value.

Scatter awards are independent from MultiWay Xtra™ awards and are also added to the total amount paid.

Awards are shown in currency.

Regardless of the bet size, there is an award cap on any single transaction. See the paytable for details. A transaction includes the results of the free spins bonus plus the outcome which launched the bonus. If the award cap is reached in the bonus, the bonus ends immediately irrespective of any remaining free spins.

Additional Information
Expected Payback
In accordance with fair gaming practices required in most legal jurisdictions world-wide, each and every game outcome is completely independent. The odds of getting any particular outcome are always the same.

The odds do not vary based on prior outcomes, bet size, balance size, time of day, day of week, etc. For example, if the top award is won on a given play of the game, the chances of winning the top award on the next play are exactly the same. Likewise, prior losses do not change future odds.

The expected payback reflects the theoretical return across a very large number of plays by numerous players over an extended period of time. The results for any given player over a play session can vary widely from this long-term, expected average in either direction. The fewer the number of games played, the wider the normal variance of actual payback that may be experienced.
