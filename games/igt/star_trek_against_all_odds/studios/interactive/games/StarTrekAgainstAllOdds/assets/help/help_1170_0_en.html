Story
In a desperate mission to stop the Romulan ship, Narada, from destroying Earth, the Enterprise follows the Narada and hides undetected in Titan's magnetic field. Spock and Kirk secretly beam aboard the Narada to recover the red matter, a black hole creation device. As planned, Spock finds the red matter inside the Jellyfish, a captured Vulcan ship.

Spock commandeers the Jellyfish and blasts into warp with the Narada in hot pursuit. The ships drop out of warp, and the Jellyfish turns to collide with the Narada. The Narada fires missiles at Spock, even though the Jellyfish still has the red matter aboard. The Enterprise arrives suddenly and destroys the Romulan missiles in flight, allowing Spock to escape unharmed.

Scotty beams Kirk and Spock back to the Enterprise, right before the Jellyfish collides with the Narada and explodes. The explosion of the Jellyfish releases the entire stockpile of red matter, creating a black hole that swallows the Narada.

The gravitational pull of the black hole tugs on the Enterprise, keeping it from escaping, even with its engines at warp speed. The pressure heavily damages the ship as Kirk orders Scotty to get them out of there at all costs. Scotty ejects the warp core and detonates it near the black hole, pushing the Enterprise to safety.

How To Bet
STAR TREK™ Against All Odds offers 720 ways to win for only 60 coins.

COIN VALUE
Displays the current coin value as currency. Press the left arrow (-) to decrease the coin value. Press the right arrow (+) to increase the coin value.

720 WAYS
Displays the current number of ways being played. Betting is fixed at 720 ways which costs 60 coins and also enables the Energizing Wilds feature.

SPIN
Press to spin the reels and submit the bet.

[ top ]


Auto Spin Feature
Auto Spin plays the game automatically for a given number of games at 720 ways and the current coin value. Once the desired coin value has been selected, press AUTO SPIN and choose how many games to play. Auto Spin plays the specified number of games until the STOP button is pressed, the Bonus is triggered, or the balance is insufficient to spin again.

Auto Spin is only available in approved jurisdictions.

MultiWay Xtra™ Feature
With the MultiWay Xtra™ feature, there are 720 ways to win awards on every spin. MultiWay Xtra™ awards pay for matching symbols in any position in adjacent columns, starting from the leftmost column or the rightmost column. More than one matching symbol in the same column multiplies the award. The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except for the Scatter and Bonus symbols. Only the highest paying MultiWay Xtra™ award for each symbol is paid per spin.

Example of a MultiWay Xtra™ payout:
This following diagram shows a sample reel outcome, focusing only on the Spock symbol. Because there is at least one Spock symbol in each of the first four columns, the player has won the 4 Spock MultiWay Xtra™ award which pays 50 coins per way. To determine the payout for this combination, count the number of Spock symbols in each column. For example, the second column has 2 Wild symbols in it, and the third column has 3 Wild symbols in it. In both cases, Wild symbols substitute for the Spock symbol. Next, multiply those counts together to obtain the number of winning ways. In this example, that would be 1 x 2 x 3 x 1 = 6 winning ways. Finally, multiply the number of winning ways times the payout for the 4 Spock MultiWay Xtra™ award. In this example, that would be 6 x 50 = 300 coins won. The actual amount won in currency would be based on the coin value.

Energizing Wilds Feature
The Energizing Wilds Feature may trigger randomly after any reel spin, except when a Scatter award is won, the Enterprise Defender Bonus is triggered, or one or more Wild symbols are visible on the reels. When the Feature triggers, the Transporter Cursor appears and randomly turns 2 or more reel symbols Wild on the three middle reels, up to a maximum of 5 additional Wild symbols.

Wins paid based on the final reel state only.

Enterprise Defender Bonus
Ready to defend the Enterprise and attain the rank of Captain?

Trigger the Enterprise Defender Bonus by getting five Bonus symbols anywhere on the reels.

Press START BATTLE and defend the Enterprise against incoming Romulan missiles for 45 seconds. Use the cursor to aim the Enterprise weapon which fires automatically. Incoming Romulan missiles may split into a number of Romulan bomblets. Missiles and bomblets damage the Enterprise shields and suspend the Enterprise weapon fire momentarily. If shields are reduced to zero, the shooting ends early. Earn points by destroying missiles and bomblets.

Item Points
Romulan missile 500
Romulan bomblet 100


Rank Score Needed
Captain 14,000+
Commander 12,000+
Lieutenant 0+


Based on earned rank, spin a random wheel for that rank. There are nine wheels total, three wheels per rank. Higher ranked wheels have higher top awards, but every wheel has the same average payback. Press SKIP TO WHEEL to skip the battle and spin a random wheel.

Earn one spin of the chosen wheel. If the first spin lands on a value, this becomes the final award. If the first spin lands on a special feature slice, a second spin is awarded.

Rank Wheel Description
Captain Wheel slices can award a second spin with two wheel pointers, two 2X wheel pointers, or two 3X wheel pointers.
Captain Wheel values are randomly boosted by 7X, 10X, or 15X before the spin.
Captain Wheel slices can award a second spin with two, three, or four wheel pointers. On the second spin, collect one to nine shields for a special shield award in addition to any other awards indicated by a pointer.
Commander Wheel slices can award a second spin with two, three, or six wheel pointers.
Commander Wheel values are randomly boosted by 5X, 7X, or 10X before the spin.
Commander Land on a shield slice to collect the number of shields on the slice and spin a second time. Win a special shield award based on the number of shields collected (up to eight shields) across both spins. This shield award is paid in addition to any awards indicated by a wheel pointer.
Lieutenant Wheel slices can award a second spin with two, three, or four wheel pointers.
Lieutenant Wheel values are randomly boosted by +1000, +2500, or +5000 before the spin.
Lieutenant Land on a shield slice to collect the number of shields on the slice and spin a second time. Win a special shield award based on the number of shields collected (up to six shields) across both spins. This shield award is paid in addition to any awards indicated by a wheel pointer.


All awards in the Bonus multiplied by the Bonus triggering coin value.

Rules and Additional Information
Misuse and malfunction voids all pays and plays.

MultiWayXtra™ awards pay for the same symbol in any position in adjacent columns.

Same symbol in same column multiplies award.

MultiWay Xtra™ awards pay left to right and right to left.

Only the highest paying MultiWay Xtra™ award for each symbol is paid per spin.

MultiWay Xtra™ awards are multiplied by the coin value.

Wild symbol only appears on reels 2, 3, and 4.

Only the highest scatter pay award is paid. Scatter pays are multiplied by the total bet.

Scatter awards are independent from MultiWay Xtra™ awards and are also added to the total amount paid.

Awards are shown in currency.

Regardless of the bet size, there is an award cap on any single transaction. See the paytable for details. A transaction includes the results of the Bonus plus the outcome which launched the Bonus.

No Federation vessels were disabled or destroyed in the making of this game.
