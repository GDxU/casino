Introduction
Enter the snow-capped forest of fortune in Siberian Storm™.

Siberian Storm™ features popular MultiWay Xtra™ wagering, with up to 720 ways to win on an eye-catching hexagonal 5-reel interface. Additionally, the game pays in both directions—left-to-right and right-to-left—leading to even more ways to win! Scatter pays and stacks of wilds make this game a payout beast!

Trigger the Free Spins Bonus by getting a Bonus symbol in any position on five consecutive reels. Stacked bonus symbols can multiply your initial 8 free spins to be 16 spins, 32 spins, or even higher. The Bonus reels are super-rich offering more stacks of wilds, and the chance to retrigger the Bonus multiple times.

Be captivated by Far East tigers and experience the rich adventure of Siberian Storm™.

How To Bet
Siberian Storm™ features 720 ways to win. You can choose your coin value, which multiplies all MultiWay Xtra™ awards won. You can also use Auto Spin to play the game for you.

COIN VALUE
Displays the current coin value as currency. Click the left arrow (with a minus sign) to decrease the coin value. Click the right arrow (with a plus sign) to increase the coin value. Clicking the left and right arrows does not necessarily decrease or increase your coin value in a linear fashion. Rather, your coin value decreases or increases at preset increments which represent the most popular bet amounts.

720 WAYS
Displays current number of ways being played. Betting is fixed at 720 ways which costs 50 coins.

SPIN
Click to spin the reels and submit your bet.

Auto Spin Feature
Auto Spin plays the game automatically for a given number of games at 720 ways and your current coin value. Once you have selected your desired coin value, click AUTO SPIN and choose how many games you want to play. Auto Spin will keep playing the specified number of games until you click the STOP button, you trigger a Bonus, or your balance is insufficient to spin again. Auto Spin is only available in approved jurisdictions.

MultiWay Xtra™ Feature
With the MultiWay Xtra™ Feature, you have 720 ways to win MultiWay Xtra™ awards on every spin. MultiWay Xtra™ awards pay for matching symbols in any position in three or more adjacent columns, starting from the leftmost column or the rightmost column. More than one matching symbol in the same column multiplies the award. The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except for the Scatter and Bonus symbols. Only the highest paying MultiWay Xtra™ award for each symbol is paid per spin.

Example of a MultiWay Xtra™ payout:
The following diagram shows a sample reel outcome, focusing only on the Tiger symbols. Since there is at least one Tiger symbol in each of the first four columns, the player has won the 4 Tiger MultiWay Xtra™ award which pays 75 coins per way. To determine the payout for this combination, count the number of Tiger symbols in each column. For example, the second column has 2 Wild symbols in it, and the third column has 3 Wild symbols in it. In both cases, Wild symbols substitute for Tiger symbols. Next, multiply those counts together which equals the number of winning ways. In this example, that would be 1 x 2 x 3 x 1 = 6 winning ways. Finally, multiply the number of winning ways times the payout for the 4 Tiger MultiWay Xtra™ award. In this example, that would be 6 x 75 = 450 coins won. The actual amount won in currency would be based on the coin value.

Free Spins Bonus
You can trigger the Free Spins Bonus by getting a Bonus symbol in any position on five consecutive reels. Each winning combination of five Bonus symbols pays a prize and awards 8 free spins. Since there are Stacked Bonus symbols, it's possible to earn up to 96 free spins initially.

The reels used in the Free Spins Bonus are much richer, having more stacks of wilds than the base game reels.

During the Free Spins Bonus, you can retrigger the Bonus by getting a Bonus Retrigger symbol in any position on five consecutive reels. Each winning combination of five Bonus Retrigger symbols pays a prize and awards 8 additional free spins. You can retrigger the Bonus multiple times, up to a maximum of 240 free spins per Bonus.

Your coin value in the Bonus is equal to the coin value on the reel spin that triggered the Bonus.

Options
Graphics Quality
Adjust the quality of the graphics to get the optimal animation performance.
  • BEST mode - Graphics look best, but performance may suffer.
  • HIGH mode (default) - Optimal graphics and performance setting.
  • MEDIUM mode - Lower quality but increased performance.
  • LOW mode - Degraded graphics, but smoothest animation performance is achieved even on slower computers.

Rules
Malfunction voids all pays and plays.

MultiWay Xtra™ awards pay for the same symbol in any position in adjacent columns. Same symbol in same column multiplies award.

MultiWay Xtra™ awards pay left to right and right to left.

Only the highest paying MultiWay Xtra™ award for each symbol is paid per spin.

MultiWay Xtra™ awards multiplied by the coin value.

Wild symbol appears on reels 2, 3, and 4 in the base game.

Wild symbol appears on reels 2, 3, and 4 in the Free Spins Bonus.

Only highest scatter pay award paid. Scatter pays are multiplied by the total bet.

Scatter awards are independent from MultiWay Xtra™ awards and are also added to the total amount paid.

Awards are shown in currency.

Regardless of your bet size, there is an award cap on any single transaction. See the paytable for details. A transaction includes the results of the Free Spins Bonus plus the outcome which launched the bonus. If the award cap is reached in the Bonus, the Bonus ends immediately irrespective of any remaining free spins.
