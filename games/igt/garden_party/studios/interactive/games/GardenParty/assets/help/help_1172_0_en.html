How To Bet
COIN VALUE
Displays the current coin value as currency. Click the left arrow (-) to decrease the coin value. Click the right arrow (+) to increase the coin value.

1024 WAYS
Displays the current number of ways to win. Betting is fixed at 1024 ways which costs 50 coins.

SPIN
Click to spin the reels and submit your bet.

Auto Spin Feature
Auto Spin plays the game automatically for a selected number of spins at your current coin value and with 1024 ways to win on each spin. Select your coin value, click AUTO SPIN and choose how many games you want to play. Auto Spin plays the specified number of games until you click the STOP button, you trigger a Bonus, or your balance is insufficient to spin again.

Auto Spin is only available in approved jurisdictions.

MultiWay Xtra™ Feature
With the MultiWay Xtra™ feature, you have 1024 ways to win on every spin. MultiWay Xtra™ symbol awards pay for matching symbols in any position in adjacent columns, starting from the leftmost column. More than one matching symbol in the same column multiplies the award. The Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except for the Bonus Gnome Scatter Pay symbol. One or more Wild symbols in a winning way doubles that winning way. Winning ways can only be doubled once in this manner.

Example of a MultiWay Xtra™ payout:
The following diagram shows a sample reel outcome, using the Butterfly symbol. Because there is at least one Butterfly or Wild symbol in each of the first four columns, the player wins the 4 Butterfly symbol award, which pays 50 coins per way.

Payout example:

Column 1 Column 2 Column 3 Column 4 Column 5
  Butterfly      
Butterfly   Butterfly Butterfly  
  Wild      
    Wild    
1 Butterfly 2 Butterflies 2 Butterflies 1 Butterfly

To determine the payout for this combination:
  1. Count the total number of winning ways (1 x 2 x 2 x 1 = 4).
  2. Count the number of non-wild winning ways (1 x 1 x 1 x 1 = 1).
  3. Calculate the number of wild winning ways (4 - 1 = 3).
  4. One or more Wild symbols in a way doubles the award. Multiply the number of wild winning ways by 2 (3 x 2 = 6).
  5. Add the number of non-wild winning ways with the number of wild winning ways (1 + 6 = 7). This example reel outcome will pay the 4 Butterfly symbol award times 7.
  6. Calculate the award amount in currency by multiplying the award (50 coins) by the coin value. For example if the coin value is £0.02 then the award is £1.00 (50 x £0.02).
  7. The total pay is the award value times the total number of winning ways (£1.00 x 7 = £7.00).
The win message will be: MULTIWAY XTRA™ WIN PAYS: £1.00 X 7

Garden Gnome Bonus
You trigger the Garden Gnome Bonus with three or more Garden Gnome symbols anywhere on the reels.

Triggering the Garden Gnome Bonus gives you the opportunity to choose the number of free spins and the maximum multiplier. Choose from the following Bonus options:
5 Free Spins with up to a 20x multiplier
10 Free Spins with up to a 15x multiplier
15 Free Spins with up to a 10x multiplier
20 Free Spins with up to a 5x multiplier
30 Free Spins with up to a 3x multiplier

Retrigger the Bonus with three or more Garden Gnome symbols anywhere on the reels. Each time you retrigger the Bonus you get to choose the number of additional free spins and the maximum multiplier for the additional Bonus round. You can retrigger the Bonus up to a maximum of three times in the same game.

The coin value in the Bonus is equal to the coin value on the reel spin that triggered the Bonus.

Rules
Misuse and malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Symbol awards pay for the same symbol in any position in adjacent columns, beginning with the far left reel.

Only the highest paying symbol award for each symbol is paid per spin. Same winning symbol in same column multiplies award. Symbol awards are multiplied by the coin value.

One or more Wild symbols completing a symbol win double its pay.

Only the highest scatter pay award is paid. Scatter pays are multiplied by the coin value.

Scatter awards are independent from symbol awards and are also added to the total amount paid.

Awards are shown in currency.

Regardless of your bet size, there is an award cap on any single transaction. See the paytable for details. A transaction includes the results of the Free Spins Bonus plus the outcome which launched the bonus. If the award cap is reached in the Bonus, the Bonus ends immediately irrespective of any remaining free spins.
