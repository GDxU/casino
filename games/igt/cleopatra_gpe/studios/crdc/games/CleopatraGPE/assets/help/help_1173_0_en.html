How To Bet
Cleopatra™ features 20 paylines. Choose to play 1, 5, 9, 15, or 20 lines and how much to bet per line.

LINE BET
Click the minus (-) button to decrease the line bet. Click the plus (+) button to increase the line bet.

LINES
Click the left arrow (-) to decrease the number of paylines played. Click the right arrow (+) to increase the number of paylines played.

SPIN
Click to spin the reels and submit the bet.

Auto Spin Feature
Auto Spin plays the game automatically for a selected number of spins at the current total bet. Select the desired total bet, click AUTO SPIN and choose how many games to play. Auto Spin will keep playing the specified number of games until you click the STOP button, you trigger a Bonus, or your balance is insufficient to spin again.

Auto Spin is only available in approved jurisdictions.

[ top ]


Cleopatra™ Bonus
Trigger the Cleopatra™ Bonus by getting three or more Sphinx symbols anywhere on the reels. Triggering the Cleopatra™ Bonus awards 15 free spins. All awards, except the 5 Cleopatra symbols, are tripled in the Free Spins Bonus.

If three or more Sphinx symbols appear anywhere on the reels during a free spin, an additional 15 free spins are added to the current Free Spins Bonus (up to a maximum of 180 total free spins within any bonus round).

Bet per line in the Bonus is equal to the bet per line on the reel spin that triggered the Bonus.

Rules
Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

All line pay symbols must appear on a played line and on consecutive reels, beginning with the far left reel.

Only highest winner paid on each payline. Line wins are multiplied by the line bet on the winning line.

Only highest scatter pay award paid. Scatter pays are multiplied by the total bet.

Coinciding wins on different paylines are added. Scatter awards are independent from payline awards and are also added to the total amount paid.

Awards are shown in currency.

Regardless of bet size, there is an award cap on any single transaction. See the paytable for details. A transaction includes the results of the Free Spins Bonus plus the outcome which launched the bonus. If the award cap is reached in the Bonus, the Bonus ends immediately irrespective of any remaining free spins.
